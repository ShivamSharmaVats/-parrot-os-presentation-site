Fast Steps

  1. Open the official Parrot download page.
  2. Pick Security or Home edition.
  3. Download the ISO and verify checksums/signatures.
  4. Write ISO to USB with Rufus, BalenaEtcher, or Ventoy.
  5. Boot from USB, test live mode, then install.
